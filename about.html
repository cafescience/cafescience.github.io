    • About Me

    My approach to user experience draws heavily from my background as a physics teacher and lab scientist. The teaching part of my background has instilled my conviction that the most important aspect of any design is that the message and tasks are communicated clearly and concisely to the user. My experience as a scientist has taught me to recognize any assumptions that are being made and the importance of testing these to make course corrections early in the process. When I'm not designing, you can find me on the ultimate frisbee field, attending science talks and museuming, or seeing death metal shows.
